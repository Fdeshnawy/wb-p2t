Post2team is a SAAS centralized employee communication tool targeting all types of employees: white-collars, blue-collars and field-force, with the ability to measure engagement & feedback.

"74% of employees feel they are missing out on internal company news." - Gallup

Why use

Improve Alignment

Generally, most of team members are unaware of organization strategy and policies. Share strategy and major KPIs with team members to improve their alignment with organization directions.

Build Trust

Build trust between you and your team members by increasing the transparency, giving access to more information, open new and simple communication channels and encourage feedback.

Increase Engagement

Engagement is the main encouraging reason for team members. It is the key to lower rates of turnover, improved satisfaction and increased loyalty.

Build Positive Environment

Feeling engaged and loyal to the organization builds a positive environment. Post2Team help more frequent knowledge sharing and prevents roomers and negative talks.

Improve Brand Image

By sharing brand identity and organization messages to team members, and allowing them to share marketing messages on the social media, team members become ambassadors to your brand.

Better Crisis Management

In times of urgent organization situations, Post2Team is a simple and quick reach to your employees anytime anywhere.

Reduce Security Risks

With security breaches and cyber-attacks everywhere, team members need to be familiar with the risks they are facing. Sharing Security information, changes in security policies and mandatory updates help keeping security at higher levels.

Encourage Feedback

Post2Team provides team members with different tools to give their feedback. They may like and/or dislike posts, in addition to sending feedback comments to the poster. They may also share the posts on social media at their convenience

Five steps to engage your team

Push2Team

Setup your organization chart

  • Build your Organization Heirarchy using organizational units
  • Upload team members data using excel sheets into organizational units
  • Build your groups Heirarchy
  • Assign team members to groups
  • Remove team members from organizational units and/or groups
  • Always track who have already used Post2Team or not

Post2Team

Select your target team members & Post

  • Select your target organizational units/groups/members
  • Create post including text, links, images and/or videos
  • Send Post

Instant Notification

Notifications and post viewing

  • Notifications sent to selected members’ mobile phones.
  • Members browse application feed and view the new post.
  • Members may like/dislike the post.
  • If allowed they can share the post on social media.
  • They may send comments privately to sender.

Engagement dashboard

Measure Engagement and feedback from analysis dashboard

  • Number of Views
  • Percentage of Likes
  • Percentage of Dislikes
  • Percentage of Shares on social media
  • Read Comments

Post2Team

Repost or Push Reminder

  • Repost to ensure highest level of engagement and confirm importance of any post
  • Push Reminder to those who have not viewed the post

Awesome Features

Web & Mobile Interface

Post2Team includes an administration and dashboard Web interface and simple mobile interface for team members similar to most social media platforms.

Simple On-boarding Process

Build organization hierarchy; build your groups’ hierarchy and upload your team members using excel sheets

Unlimited Scalability

Whether you are a small or a large organization, Post2Team can help you reach your team with no restrictions

Group Members

Divide your team members into groups. Any team member can be a member of multiple groups at the same time.

Only authorized people may post.

To avoid the noise, only authorized users are allowed to post on Post2Team.

Target Team Members

Post your messages only to targeted team members. Every member will only be notified of posts that target him to reduce noise caused by other messaging tools.

Any type of Information

Post2Team supports text, links, images and videos to help you communicate any type of information to your team members.

Measure Engagement

Measure how many of the targeted team members view each post and analyse them by organization unit and group.

Track feedback

Team members may like, dislike and share each post. They may also send comments privately. Using sender dashboard, senders track and analyse members feedback.

Re-Notify

To ensure successful receipt of information, senders may resend posts to all recipients and/or to those who have not viewed it.

Instant Notification

Members receive instant notifications on their smart phone as soon as they are targeted with any post.

Repository of organization messages

Post2Team is a one place to follow all organization communication. You may also search in historical posts to reach old one easily

Supports all types of information

Post2Team supports all types of information.
